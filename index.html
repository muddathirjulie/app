About Me

Hello, I’m Muddathir Julie — a Data Engineer specialising in building scalable, production-grade data systems and software solutions. I currently work at Cipla, where I contribute to data pipelines, reporting, and analytics that support business decision-making.

With a background in software development and data analytics, I design and implement end-to-end data solutions — from ingestion and transformation to reporting and application layers — ensuring data is reliable, accessible, and actionable.
