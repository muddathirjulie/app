About Me

Hello, I’m Muddathir Julie — a Junior Data Engineer with a background in Software Engineering and Data Analysis. Welcome to my digital portfolio, where I transform complex data into actionable insights and build systems that drive smarter decisions.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, majoring in Software Engineering, from Eduvos (May 2023). My academic journey built my foundation in data structures, algorithms, and application development — with a growing focus on data-centric solutions.

I began my career as a Full Stack Developer Intern at Digital2DNA (Feb–Aug 2023), where I worked across both front-end and back-end systems. Later, I joined Creed Living Pty (Ltd) as a Data Analyst, extracting insights from complex datasets and supporting strategic business decisions.

In November 2024, I became a permanent member of the Creed Living team, continuing to lead data initiatives and manage the company's website. Along the way, I’ve also developed internal tools like a costing system and expiry tracker to improve team efficiency.

Passionate about building robust data infrastructure, I specialize in designing ETL pipelines using SQL, Python, Firebase, and Power BI — delivering scalable solutions for real-world impact. I also hold certifications in Power BI, Web Development, and WordPress eCommerce.

Outside of work, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects and engaging with community-driven tech initiatives. I believe in using technology not just for productivity — but for purpose.
