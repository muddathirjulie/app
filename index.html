About Me

Hello, I am Muddathir Julie, a dedicated Junior Software Engineer and Data Analyst. Welcome to my digital portfolio, where I aim to create sophisticated solutions to intricate challenges and explore the limitless potential of technology.


I graduated in May 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology degree, majoring in Software Engineering from Eduvos, which gave me a strong foundation in software development methodologies, algorithms, and data structures. During my studies, I engaged in various projects, from creating dynamic web and mobile applications to solving complex algorithmic problems, deepening my technical expertise and igniting my passion for technology.


I gained valuable experience as a Full Stack Developer Intern at Digital2DNA from February to August 2023, where I honed my skills in both front-end and back-end development. In November 2023, I joined Creed Living Pty (Ltd) as a Data Analyst on a one-year contract, where I leveraged my analytical skills to extract insights and drive data-informed decisions. As of November 2024, I am now a permanent team member at Creed Living, where I also work on managing and optimizing the company's website for performance and user experience.


Beyond my professional and academic pursuits, I am passionate about community engagement and contributing to open-source projects. I believe in the transformative power of technology to drive positive change and am eager to be involved in initiatives that make a meaningful impact.
